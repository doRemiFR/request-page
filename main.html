FR Commissions

This is a guide on how to use the following request page.

  1. Input your Username
  2. Include any references. Any requests without references will result in an additional design fee
  3. (Optional) Include any information about your character.
  4. Select your method of Payment

Commissions are drawn on a canvas of 500 x 500 pixels.

Prices are per character, not per image.

Prices may vary every holiday
